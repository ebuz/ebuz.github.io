About Me

I am a PhD Student in Brain and Cognitive Sciences at the University of Rochester. I study language. My interests fall into two categories 1) understanding the planning process of language production and 2) grammatical structure variation and how it arises.

I am a member of HLP and use corpus and experimental methods to study the aforementioned areas.
